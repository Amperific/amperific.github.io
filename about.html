Manifesto

With 3 years in Android Linux Kernel Developement, I tackle a broad variety of setbacks in which an Android device lacks of in its backend Operating System mechanisms. With carefully selected focusing points and skills I express my visions from kernel optimizations to driver restructuring with excellence.

Through design thinking, I am able to pinpoint major vulnerabilities in performance, rectify the issue and evolve it by adding new software features and innovations that meet user needs as well as my personal skills development. Besides custom build the Android Linux Kernel, I am venturing forth in Mobile Application Development. This helps to constantly push my forward so as to strive for excellence and to be able to emphasize a user-centered approach in all of my work. I work with the latest techniques in web development and my agile workflow is the result of the precise strategies that include advanced pre-processors, scripts and objects. With my knowledge in backend development and web development, I foster productive teams in a collaborative environment that meets the requirements of creatives and developers at the same time.
